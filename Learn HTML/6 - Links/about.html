The plot of The Two Towers


The Fellowship is torn apart. Sauron's power is growing stronger. While venturing on to Mordor,
Frodo and Sam come across the grotesque creature Gollum, who is forever consumed by the Ring's power.
Meanwhile, Aragorn, Gandalf, Legolas and Gimli meet up with new allies in the Plains of Rohan. Together,
they must work to defend the people of Rohan against Sauron's puppet Saruman as he unleashes his armies from Isengard.
All the while, Sauron prepares to launch an attack on Gondor. The War for the Ring begins here.
